▸ ABOUT THIS PROJECT

The working group

The Collision Horizon is a collaborative forecast, written by practitioners rather than a single author. It exists to be argued with — a shared, evolving position on how artificial intelligence reshapes the marketing industry between now and the end of 2033.

Every date in the forecast is a hypothesis. The working group's job is to pressure-test those hypotheses against evidence, lived experience and disagreement — and to keep the document honest as the technology and the market move.

IN PARTNERSHIP WITH

Produced in partnership with AMA Chicago — the Chicago chapter of the American Marketing Association — convening voices from across the industry to debate what AI means for the future of the profession.
